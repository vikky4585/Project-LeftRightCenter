PERSONALITY POLITICS

Since the 2016 American presidential election, it seems as though US national politics have become increasingly tribal, with people aligning with candidates that reflect their outlook rather than candidates that reflect specific policy views. With that lens, we wanted to know if there are differences Democrat and Republican personalities? How big are these differences? Are there personality traits that are "Republican" or "Democrat"? If so, what are these charatersitics? And finally, can we predict your politics based on your personality traits?

To answer this question, we used a nataral language processor (LIWC) to analyzed 257,000 tweets from the outgoing US senate. Using these tweets, we looked at the differences between Democrats and Republicans and explored to what extent you can predict whether a politician is a Democrat or Republican based on the personality traits of their Twitter feed. The analysis includes:

  1. A description of the charteristics of Democrats and Republicans
  2. Analysis of differences both within parties and between parties
  3. Understanding how accurately you can predict whether a politician (or a person) is a Democrat or Repbulican
  4. Predicting whether someone is likely a Democrat or a Reublican based on their twitter feed
  5. Interactive graphics showing how Democratic and Republican congressmen skew on each feature
  6. Layer on the election results and compare the three map data
  7. Host the findings using Flask

Tools

  1. Python - Flask, Pymongo, Tweepy
  2. Machine Learning - SkLearn, Keras, Neural Networks, RandomForestClassifier, SVM
  3. JS Libraries - Plotly, D3, JQuery
  4. Front End - HTML, Bootstrap

Analysis

Using the twitter api, we pulled 250,000 tweest from conngressmembers. We then used the LIWC api understand what their personatity was based on their twitter feeds. From there, we analyzed the personility traits of our nations leaders, understanding what is the overall personality of the congress and how democrats and republicans differ. Next, we used machine learning (Random Forests) to understand to what extent you can predict whether someone is democrat or republican based on their twitter feed

Limitations & Considerations:

The personalities based on twitter may not be representative of a person's overall personality

  • We believe that the personality of the twitter feeds is based on which party is in power, and several traits may change based on which party is in power
  • We are limited by the timeframe - this may changed if looked at over shorter or longer timeframes
  • Other factors, such whether a candidate is running for re-election, as time in office, region and size of twitter following could influence personality
